About

Making finance & technology make sense.

Our mission, in one page.

Capital & Code exists for a simple reason: the most important shifts in money — markets, artificial intelligence, and the fintech rewiring how we pay, save, and borrow — are usually explained badly. Too much jargon, too much hype, not enough clarity.

We write the version we wish existed: clear, practical, and honest about what's genuinely useful versus what's just noise.

What we cover

  • Finance — the timeless fundamentals, from compound interest to how markets actually work.
  • AI — where machine learning is really used in money, minus the science fiction.
  • Fintech — the companies and technology reshaping everyday financial life.

Our principles

Explain it like you respect the reader's intelligence but not their patience for jargon.

Every article aims to leave you with one thing: a concept you now understand well enough to explain to someone else.

A note on advice

We publish education, not personalized financial advice. Always do your own research and consider speaking to a qualified professional before making money decisions.
